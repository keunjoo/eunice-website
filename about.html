Hi, I'm Eunice.

I am a fourth-year Cognitive Science major at UC Berkeley, with a passion for functional aesthetics. I believe that empathy is the key component of good design, and good design is an equilateral triangle of functionality, aesthetics and user experience. I am always intrigued by human cognition and behavior, and this curiosity translates into my appetite for that perfect, equilateral triangle.

Design and academics aside, I like exploring new cafes and bars, going on adventures in the city, and watching movies multiple times (I watched Inception 8 times and The Shawshank Redemption 4 times.). I am particularly obsessed with sunsets, nighttimes and cityscapes—any combination of these is enough to get me extremely excited. I’ve been working on a (very) long-term project of plowing through IMDb’s Top 250 movies list.
