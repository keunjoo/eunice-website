TidyUp

A chores checklist app concept I designed for fun. I got the inspiration while my housemate and I were making a chores spreadsheet, dividing up tasks and figuring out how often we should do them. It's nice to give your housemate a reminder from time to time, so I added a reminder feature by which you can send a notification to the housemate who is responsible for a particular task.
