Hi, I'm Eunice,

a designer.

I am a recent UC Berkeley grad with a degree in Cognitive Science and a passion for functional aesthetics. I believe that empathy is the key component of good design, and good design is an equilateral triangle of functionality, aesthetics and user experience. I am always intrigued by human cognition and behavior, and this curiosity translates into my appetite for that perfect, equilateral triangle.

Design aside, I like exploring new cafes and bars, going on adventures in the city, and watching movies multiple times (I watched Inception 9 times and The Shawshank Redemption 4 times.). I am particularly obsessed with sunsets, nighttimes and cityscapes—any combination of these is enough to get me extremely excited. I’ve been working on a (very) long-term project of plowing through IMDb’s Top 250 movies list.
