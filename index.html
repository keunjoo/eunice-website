I am always intrigued by human cognition and behavior, and this curiosity translates into my appetite for that perfect, equilateral triangle.

Design aside, I like exploring new cafes and bars, scavenging on SoundCloud and Apple Music for new music, and watching movies multiple times (I watched Inception 9 times and The Shawshank Redemption 4 times. Ex Machina comes next.). I am particularly obsessed with sunsets, nighttimes and cityscapes—any combination of these is enough to get me extremely excited.
